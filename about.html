PARKHI MALHOTRA


I see design as an essential part that contributes to the character and depth to build a product and has always admired how thoughtful design thinking has the power to shape our experiences. I am very interested in design and solving problems in any way possible by thinking design. In the past few months, I’ve loved talking to people about their experiences and writing about them. I love what I do because it makes me feel more connected to humans. I am very much interested in the research part of the process and eager to work on projects that are challenging and thought-provoking.

When I am not working, I enjoy reading, writing, and exploring new places. So, aside from design, I read and write in no particular order and binge-watch episodes of The Office.
